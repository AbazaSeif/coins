About Me

David D'Agostino

Cryptocurrency Enthusiast

I am a software engineer living in Washington D.C. who has grown obsessed with cryptocurrency ever since reading about Bitcoin back in 2011. After realizing I was constantly checking the price of bitcoin and researching the never ending flow of new alt coins on a daily basis, I decided to create this site. Using Angular 4, Material Kit Pro and PHP, I have built a single page application to satisfy both my itch to code and my curiosity for cryptocurrenies. Please check back often as I plan to iterate and add features over time as well as refining the design.
